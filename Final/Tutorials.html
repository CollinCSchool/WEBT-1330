How to Create a Sculpture

Step 1

First, open your program of choice. In this case, I will be using Blender! You will see a default scene assuming your preferences are set to the default.

Step 2

Next, you want to delete the default cube and everything else. To do this, press "A" to select everything in the scene and the press the delete key "del" on your numpad. Alternatively you can press "X" once you select everything and select "Delete" when prompted. Your scene should look like mine now!

Step 3

Once your scene is empty, you want to press "Shift+A" to add a new shape. Add a UV Sphere. Next run a common script by pressing the hotkey(s) "Alt+F4" to automatically sculpt the UV Sphere into a randomly generated bust!
